# QC Project Proposal: Building the "Chinese Cigarette Museum" – A Safe, Compliant, and In-Depth Tobacco Knowledge Platform

## 1. Background and Significance

Currently, information about the Chinese tobacco market is fragmented, lacking a centralized, accurate, and regulatory-compliant digital knowledge platform. Existing platforms like Yanyue.cn and ETMOC.com either focus on community interaction or industry news, falling short in terms of **data systematicity, purity, and cultural depth**.

The "Chinese Cigarette Museum" project aims to create a **comprehensive platform that integrates a product database with historical and cultural exhibits**. We will not only systematically organize core data (specifications, pricing, etc.) for over 3,200 tobacco products but also delve into the developmental histories of key brands and manufacturers to enrich its cultural narrative. The platform adheres to the principles of **purity, professionalism, and being ad-free**, strictly complies with national laws and regulations, and is dedicated to becoming an authoritative window for tobacco knowledge dissemination and cultural heritage.

## 2. Project Objectives

1.  **Build a Core Database**: Establish and maintain an online database of 3,200+ SKUs of Chinese tobacco products (cigarettes, cigars, e-cigarettes), ensuring data accuracy and clear structure.
2.  **Enrich Cultural Content**: Systematically compile and present the developmental journeys and cultural stories of key tobacco brands and manufacturers, adding a rich knowledge dimension to the platform.
3.  **Ensure Safety and Compliance**:
    *   Strictly adhere to the Advertising Law and tobacco-related regulations, with all content oriented towards **knowledge dissemination**.
    *   Implement an **age verification mechanism** to prevent access by minors.
    *   Prioritize official channels for data sourcing and include disclaimers.
4.  **Optimize User Experience**: Provide efficient and intuitive search and filtering functions, and build a stable, fast, modern web application.

## 3. Risk Analysis and Mitigation Strategies

| Risk Factor | Mitigation Strategy |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Regulatory Compliance Risk** | Position the platform as a non-commercial knowledge dissemination and cultural education resource; prominently display "Smoking is harmful to health" warnings; eliminate any promotional content. |
| **Data Accuracy Risk** | Prioritize official sources like the State Tobacco Monopoly Administration and China Tobacco companies; establish a regular verification and update mechanism; clearly state "Data is for reference only". |
| **Minor Access Risk** | Enforce mandatory age confirmation (18+) on first visit; restrict search engine indexing via `robots.txt`. |
| **AI Competitor Risk** | Focus on **depth and systematicity**, offering structured data comparison and historical/cultural insights that AI Q&A cannot replicate; strengthen community value and a pure, distraction-free user experience. |

## 4. Implementation Plan

1.  **Core Data and Content Development (Phase 1)**:
    *   Develop a standardized data template covering cigarette product specifications, official pricing, manufacturer info, and high-res images.
    *   Launch a special content series on "Brand/Manufacturer Histories" to uncover cultural value.
2.  **Platform Development and Security Enhancement**:
    *   Iteratively optimize the existing web app (https://tobacco-atlas.vercel.app/), integrating safety modules like age verification.
    *   Improve the data management backend to ensure standardized and efficient content updates.
3.  **Compliance Review and Community Guidance**:
    *   Establish a pre-publication compliance review process for all content.
    *   Set community guidelines to foster high-quality interactions centered on knowledge sharing and cultural discussion.
4.  **Future Feature Expansion (Phase 2)**:
    *   **Expand to Cigars**: Extend the database to include cigars and establish a dynamic mechanism for collecting and updating market retail prices.
    *   **Deepen Educational Content**: Add practical knowledge sections like "Authenticity Verification Guides" and "Cigar Storage Tips" to reinforce the platform's role in knowledge dissemination and cultural education.

## 5. Expected Outcomes

1.  **Establish a safe, compliant, and authoritative online tobacco knowledge repository**, effectively filling a market gap.
2.  **Create a unique "Data + Culture + Education" tripartite model**, establishing a differentiated advantage against AI assistants and other competitors.
3.  **Provide a pure and professional information service platform** for tobacco enthusiasts, researchers, and industry professionals, offering both practical and socio-cultural value.
4.  **Enhance users' knowledge and consumption experience** through practical features like authenticity verification and storage tips, solidifying the platform's position as a core hub for knowledge dissemination and cultural education.
